<section id="about">
  <h2>About</h2>
  <p>
    I am a postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Mathematics at the
    University of Manitoba. My postdoctoral advisor is Prof. Robert T. W.
    Martin. Prior to this, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the Technion – Israel
    Institute of Technology under the guidance of Prof. Orr Moshe Shalit.
  </p>
  <p>
    I completed my Ph.D. in Mathematics at Washington University in St. Louis
    under the supervision of Prof. Greg Knese.
  </p>
  <p>
    My research lies at the intersection of complex analysis, operator theory,
    and function theory. I am currently interested in problems arising in
    noncommutative function theory, which studies holomorphic functions in
    several noncommuting variables.
  </p>
</section>
